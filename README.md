## Project Overview

In this project, I analyzed an e-commerce sales dataset to understand sales performance, product profitability, and customer purchasing patterns. The goal was to extract meaningful insights and present them through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Tools & Technologies

The following tools and technologies were used in this project:

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Power BI
* Data Visualization

---

## Dataset

The dataset includes information related to product sales and transactions such as:

* Product Category and Sub-Category
* Sales Revenue
* Profit
* Quantity Sold
* Discount Applied
* Customer Segment
* Region of Sales

This data helps analyze how different products, regions, and customer segments contribute to overall business performance.

---

## Analysis Performed

The analysis focused on several important aspects of the business:

* Sales performance by product category
* Profitability of different product sub-categories
* Regional sales comparison
* Customer segment sales distribution
* Relationship between sales and profit

---

## Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes the following visualizations:

* KPI metrics (Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Units Sold, Average Discount)
* Sales by Category
* Profit Distribution by Category
* Top Performing Sub-Categories
* Regional Sales Comparison
* Customer Segment Sales Analysis
* Scatter Plot for Sales vs Profit

---

## Key Insights

Some important insights from the analysis include:

* The **Technology** category generates the highest revenue and profit.
* **Copiers** and **Phones** are among the most profitable sub-categories.
* **Tables** and **Bookcases** show negative profitability.
* The **West region** contributes the highest share of total sales.
* The **Consumer segment** accounts for the largest portion of revenue.
